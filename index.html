A few facts about planet earth



Earth is the third planet from the Sun, the densest planet in the Solar System, the largest of the Solar System's four terrestrial planets, and the only celestial body known to accommodate life. It is home to about 8.74 million species.[31][32] There are billions of humans[33] who depend upon its biosphere and minerals.

Top 3 earth facts

  1. Earth is a terrestrial planet, meaning that it is a rocky body, rather than a gas giant like Jupiter
  2. The interior of the Earth, like that of the other terrestrial planets, is divided into layers by their chemical or physical (rheological) properties, but unlike the other terrestrial planets, it has a distinct outer and inner core.
  3. The mechanically rigid outer layer of the Earth, the lithosphere, is broken into pieces called tectonic plates.
